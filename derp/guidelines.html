Guidelines

Post Content

The content of posts must be "family-friendly." This means that no "X-rated" or "NSFW" content will ever be permitted. Posts that feature deliberate misinformation will not be permitted. 

Misinformation

Misinformation (AKA "fake-news) is content that can be verified untrue by several news or information sites. This policy does not apply to opinionated posts.

Safety

Violent and illegal content or content that promotes illegal or violent acts is never permitted.

Sources

We ask that if you are sharing medical or law associated content that you include a source link or source name for the content (It is optional, but preferred). This is to help limit spam. Posts that lack source information may be restricted under our misinformation policy.
