About

Feed

Feed content is reviewed and voted on before going live. The team at Borg Creative Studios first reviews the post to be sure that it follows all of our guidelines. Then the post (If it follows guidelines) will then go live on the voting page where users can then vote on the post.

Voting

Voting is nice and simple: Thumbs up or thumbs down. The higher the votes, the greater chance of going onto the feed! Thumbs downs are subtracted from the total thumbs ups. The posts with the highest votes then go to the feed and the ones that don't make the cut disappear!

Mondays

Unlike other platforms, ours is not a revolving door of content. The feed is updated every Monday with the best voted posts.

Post expiration

Nothing lasts forever, even posts on BCS Social. posts from the feed last 7 days, then they expire. 

Posting

Posting on our platform is simple. Enter your email, make a username, then enter your post's text. All posts have to meet the guidelines.

Verified Users

As a way to reduce spam and fraud, users can apply to become verified. Verified users will have an easier time submitting posts.

Money

Social networks cost money to operate. It's a fact. We make money in two ways, privacy-friendly marketing and premium features.

Privacy

Your privacy is important, and that is why we do not sell your email with anyone (Unless we are ordered to by a court-of-law.) Read the policy here.
